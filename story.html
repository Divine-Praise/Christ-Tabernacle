OUR STORY

About Us

Welcome to CHRIST TABERNACLE, a place of worship, community, and spiritual growth. We are dedicated to spreading the message of love, faith, and hope in Araromi and beyond.

Our Mission

At CHRIST TABERNACLE, our Mission is simple yet profound: to inspire and guide individuals on their spiritual journeys, helping the build a deeper connection with God and each other. we aim to create an inclusive and nurturing environment where everyone can find solace, belonging, and purpose.

Our Beliefs

We are a Pentecostal Church that embraces the core tenets of Christianity. Our faith is grounded in the Holy Bible, and we believe in the power of prayer, worship, and living out the teaching of Jesus Christ

Our Community

We cherish the diverse tapestry of our congregation, welcomeing people from all walks of life, backgrounds, and ages. Whether you're a long-time believer or exploring your faith for the first time, you'll find a warm and supportive community here at CHRIST TABERNACLE.

Worship Services

Join us for inspiring worship services that include heartfelt music, engaging sermons, and a sense of spiritual renewal. We offer services on Tuesday - 10:00am to 3:00pm, Thursday - 10:00am to 2:00pm, 3rd Friday of the month is our night vigil. 11:00pm to 5:00pm, Last saturday of the month is our youth program. 1:00pm to 2:00pm, with opportunities for both in-person and online participation.
