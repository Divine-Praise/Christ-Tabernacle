WHAT WE TEACH

1. The Bible as Our Foundation:

. At CHRIST TABERNACLE, we believe that the Bible is the inspired and authoritative Word of God. It serves as our guide for faith, practice, and living a meaningful life.

2. Salvation Through Jesus Christ:

. We teach that salvation is through faith in Jesus Christ alone. His life, death, and resurrection offer forgiveness of sins and eternal life to all who believe.

3. Discipleship and Growth:

. We are committed to helping believers grow in their faith through prayer, study of Scripture, and active involvement in the church community.

4. Worship:

. Our worship services are a vital part of our spiritual life. We emphasize heartfelt worship through prayer, music, and the preaching of God's Word.

5. Community and Fellowship:

. We believe in the importance of Christian community and strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment for all members.

6. Serving Others:

. Following Jesus' example, we are dedicated to serving our local and global communities through acts of compassion and outreach.

7. Family and Relationships:

. We emphasize the importance of strong families and healthy relationships, grounded in biblical principles.

8. Moral and Ethical Standards:

. We seek to live by the moral and ethical standards set forth in the Bible, guided by love, compassion, and grace.

9. Hope and Eternity:

. Our teaching provides hope for the future and emphasizes the promise of eternal life with God for those who trust in Him.


. We invite you to explore our website for more information about our services, programs, and events. If you have any questions or would like to know more about our teachings, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. We look forward to the opportunity to connect with you and grow together in faith.
